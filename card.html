The Octopus

Giant Pacific octopuses can change color to blend in wherever they go!

  • Type:Giant Octopus
  • Weight:Adults usually weigh around 15 kg
  • Lifespan: 1 to 2 years
  • Habitat: Most octopuses stay along the ocean’s floor, although some species are pelagic, which means they live near the water’s surface.

Octopuses are sea animals famous for their rounded bodies, bulging eyes, and eight long arms. They live in all the world’s oceans but are especially abundant in warm, tropical waters
